Our Services

Comprehensive Support for Families Across Borders

Expert cross-border social work services supporting families through assessment, protection, reunification, and ongoing care across international boundaries.

Child and family assessment

Comprehensive evaluations of family dynamics, child welfare, and home environments across borders. Our trained social workers conduct thorough assessments to support informed decision-making for children's best interests and family stability.

Child Protection Inquiring

Swift and sensitive investigations into child protection concerns across international boundaries. We conduct thorough inquiries with cultural awareness, ensuring child safety while respecting family dignity and working within legal frameworks of both jurisdictions.

Supporting and Strengthening families

Proactive intervention and resource connection to build family resilience and capability. We provide culturally sensitive support, parenting guidance, and access to community resources that help families overcome challenges and maintain healthy relationships across distances.

Search for origins

Compassionate assistance in tracing family history and biological connections across international borders. We navigate complex documentation, cultural sensitivities, and legal requirements to help individuals discover their roots and reconnect with their heritage and family members.

Family Unification

Dedicated coordination to bring separated family members together across international boundaries. We facilitate communication between jurisdictions, navigate immigration processes, prepare families emotionally, and ensure smooth transitions that prioritize children's wellbeing and family bonds.

Special Guardianship Assessment

Thorough evaluation of prospective special guardians across international contexts. Our assessments examine suitability, capacity to care, family dynamics, and long-term stability to support court decisions that secure permanent, loving homes for children outside of adoption frameworks.
